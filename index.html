Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before engaging Bestcare FM.

What types of properties does Bestcare FM manage?
Bestcare FM manages commercial offices, shopping malls, hospitals, schools, industrial parks, warehouses, hotels, residential estates, banks, and government buildings across Kenya. If your property has a facility challenge, we have a solution — regardless of size or sector.

Do you offer integrated FM or individual services only?
We offer both. Clients can engage Bestcare FM for a fully integrated, single-vendor FM bundle — covering everything from cleaning to CAFM — or procure individual services such as cleaning, security, or M&E maintenance on a standalone basis. Most clients begin with one or two services and expand over time.

What is your service coverage area in Kenya?
Our primary operating zone is Nairobi County and its satellite towns — including Kiambu, Machakos, and Kajiado. We also serve Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and Eldoret. Contact us to confirm coverage for your specific location and we'll confirm availability within 24 hours.

How quickly can you mobilise after contract signing?
For standard contracts, we mobilise within 5–10 business days of signing — including staff deployment, equipment procurement, and CAFM system setup. Emergency or critical deployments can be mobilised within 24–48 hours, subject to scope and staffing availability.

Do you provide your own equipment and consumables?
Yes. Bestcare FM supplies all equipment, uniforms, PPE, and consumables required to deliver agreed services. Cleaning chemicals, pest control products, maintenance tools, and CAFM licenses are included in the monthly management fee unless otherwise specified in your contract.

What certifications and compliance standards do you hold?
Bestcare FM holds ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management), ISO 14001:2015 (Environmental Management), and ISO 45001:2018 (Occupational Health & Safety) certifications. We are also a registered BIFM affiliate and NITA-registered training provider. Copies of all certificates are available on request.

How are your services priced — fixed fee or schedule of rates?
Pricing is based on a fixed monthly management fee for planned services, plus a schedule of rates for reactive and ad-hoc works. All pricing is transparent, itemised, and agreed in writing before contract commencement — no hidden charges, no surprises at month end.

What happens if a service standard is not met?
We operate to defined SLAs. Where a standard is not met, our CAFM system flags the breach automatically and our operations team initiates corrective action within the agreed response window. Persistent SLA failures trigger a formal rectification plan and, in some contracts, service credit provisions.

How do clients track service performance and reporting?
Every client receives a dedicated CAFM dashboard with real-time job status, asset history, and SLA tracking. A formal monthly performance report — covering task completion rates, response times, and compliance scores — is delivered to your account manager by the 5th of each month.

Are your staff uniformed, ID-badged, and vetted?
Yes. All Bestcare FM staff are background-checked against KRA databases, uniformed with property-specific branding, and issued photo ID badges before deployment. We maintain a live staff register for every contract, available to clients on request at any time.

Do you provide emergency / out-of-hours response?
Yes. Our 24/7 helpdesk handles emergency calls around the clock. For critical failures — such as plumbing leaks, electrical faults, or security breaches — our 2-hour emergency response SLA applies. Out-of-hours call-outs are included in most integrated FM contracts.

How do I get a quote for my facility?
Fill in the enquiry form on this page, call us on 0709 004 600, or send us a WhatsApp message. We'll arrange a free site assessment at your property and deliver a tailored, itemised proposal within 48 hours of the visit — at no cost and with no obligation.

About Bestcare FM

Our Mission: To be Kenya's most trusted integrated facility management partner — delivering measurable service excellence that helps our clients focus on their core business while we take care of their built environment.

Our Vision: A Kenya where every commercial, industrial, and residential property operates at peak performance through professional, technology-enabled FM services that meet international standards.

Our Values:

  • Accountability: We own our SLAs. If we miss a standard, we fix it — fast.
  • Professionalism: Uniformed, trained, and ID-badged staff on every contract.
  • Innovation: We use CAFM technology and digital reporting to stay ahead.
  • Sustainability: Green cleaning products, waste diversion, and energy savings for every client.
  • Partnership: We treat every client's facility as if it were our own.

Founded in Nairobi, Bestcare FM has grown from a specialist cleaning company to Kenya's most comprehensive FM provider, managing over 500 properties and employing more than 2,000 trained staff across Nairobi and major Kenyan cities. Our leadership team brings decades of FM, property management, and engineering experience from across East Africa and the UK, ensuring international standards with local knowledge.

Every Bestcare FM contract is backed by ISO 9001:2015-certified quality management processes, a dedicated account manager, and a 24/7 helpdesk. We don't just deliver services — we deliver peace of mind.

Cleaning & Housekeeping

Bestcare FM's cleaning division operates across commercial offices, shopping malls, hospitals, schools, industrial facilities, and residential estates throughout Kenya. Our trained housekeeping teams deliver daily janitorial services, scheduled deep cleans, and specialist sanitation programmes tailored to your property type and occupancy pattern.

All cleaning staff complete a structured NITA-accredited training programme covering chemical handling, infection control, equipment operation, and customer service. We use colour-coded microfibre systems to prevent cross-contamination and employ eco-certified, biodegradable cleaning products that meet international green cleaning standards.

Every cleaning contract is supported by a daily task schedule logged in our CAFM system, so you can see exactly what was done, when, and by whom. Monthly quality audits — scored and reported — ensure standards never slip. Equipment, consumables, uniforms, and PPE are all supplied by Bestcare FM at no additional cost.

Security Management

Bestcare FM's security division provides professional manned guarding, access control management, CCTV monitoring, and incident response services for commercial, industrial, and residential properties across Kenya. All guards are licensed under the Private Security Regulatory Authority (PSRA) and complete a rigorous pre-deployment training programme.

Our security methodology is risk-based: we begin every new contract with a security survey and risk assessment, then design a deployment plan — including post positions, patrol routes, and response protocols — that matches your specific threat profile. CCTV systems are monitored in real time, with incident logs maintained in our CAFM platform and accessible to clients 24/7.

We operate a 2-hour emergency response SLA for critical security incidents. All guards are uniformed with property-specific branding, issued photo ID, and managed by a dedicated Security Supervisor who reports directly to your Bestcare FM account manager.

Mechanical & Electrical Maintenance

Our M&E maintenance team handles planned preventive maintenance (PPM) and reactive repairs for all mechanical and electrical systems across your facility — including HVAC, plumbing, generators, lifts, fire suppression systems, lighting, and electrical distribution boards.

Every asset on your site is registered in our CAFM system with a planned maintenance schedule aligned to manufacturer recommendations and NCA compliance requirements. PPM visits are logged digitally, with completion certificates and condition reports issued after every service. Reactive faults are raised via our 24/7 helpdesk and allocated to the nearest qualified technician within our agreed response SLA.

Our engineers hold relevant NCA, Kenya Power, and City & Guilds-equivalent certifications. We have helped clients reduce energy consumption by up to 18% through proactive M&E optimisation programmes — combining LED upgrades, HVAC tuning, and BMS integration into a single, cost-effective package.

Landscaping & Grounds

Bestcare FM's landscaping and grounds team manages the outdoor environments of commercial campuses, residential estates, hospitals, and industrial parks across Kenya. Services include regular lawn mowing, garden bed maintenance, hedge trimming, irrigation system management, tree pruning, and seasonal planting programmes.

Our horticulturalists design bespoke planting schemes suited to Kenya's climate zones — from the humid Nairobi highlands to the drier Rift Valley belt — using drought-resistant species where appropriate and drip irrigation systems to reduce water usage by up to 30%.

Grounds maintenance schedules are integrated into the CAFM system so clients can track visit frequency, task completion, and grounds condition scores over time. We supply all equipment, tools, fertilisers, and irrigation consumables. Hardscaping services — including paving repairs, perimeter fencing, car park marking, and external drainage — are also available on request.

Pest & Rodent Control

Bestcare FM's pest management division delivers integrated pest management (IPM) programmes that combine prevention, monitoring, and targeted treatment — minimising chemical exposure while achieving consistent pest-free results for your property.

Our certified pest control technicians are registered with the Pest Control Products Board (PCPB) of Kenya. Treatment programmes cover rodents, cockroaches, ants, flies, termites, bedbugs, and outdoor pests. All chemicals used are WHO-approved and applied following PCPB-compliant safety protocols, with full documentation provided after each visit.

For healthcare and food-service environments, we design specialised healthcare-grade programmes using gel baiting, heat treatment, and exclusion techniques that eliminate pests without disrupting sensitive operations. Monthly inspection reports and pest activity trend analysis help clients understand root causes and implement structural prevention measures for long-term results.

Waste Management

Bestcare FM's waste management service covers the full waste lifecycle — from segregation at source to collection, transportation, and verified disposal or recycling. We implement colour-coded waste segregation systems at your facility, train your staff on waste classification, and coordinate regular collection with licensed waste handlers across Kenya.

Our waste programmes are designed to align with the Environmental Management and Co-ordination Act (EMCA) and NEMA regulations. For clients with sustainability targets, we provide monthly waste diversion reports showing total waste generated, waste recycled, and waste-to-landfill ratios — giving you the data you need for ESG and NEMA compliance reporting.

Specialist waste streams — including clinical waste from healthcare facilities, e-waste, and hazardous chemical waste — are handled through certified specialist disposal partners with full chain-of-custody documentation. Our goal: zero preventable waste reaching landfill by 2030.

Catering & Pantry Services

Bestcare FM's catering division provides corporate catering, pantry management, and vending solutions for offices, industrial facilities, and institutional clients across Kenya. Our trained catering teams manage everything from daily breakfast and lunch services to boardroom catering for executive meetings and large-scale event hospitality.

We design menus around your workforce demographics, dietary preferences, and budget parameters — with daily meal planning that rotates proteins, vegetables, and carbohydrates to support employee wellbeing. All food handlers are certified under Kenya's Food, Drugs and Chemical Substances Act, with food safety audits conducted quarterly.

Pantry management services include daily restocking of beverages, snacks, and essentials, dishwasher operation, and pantry hygiene maintenance. Vending machine installation, restocking, and maintenance are also available. Monthly catering reports cover meal counts, food cost per head, and compliance scores.

Parking & Traffic Control

Bestcare FM's parking and traffic management service ensures safe, efficient, and organised vehicle movement at your facility — whether you manage a 50-bay office car park or a 2,000-space mall parking deck. Our uniformed parking marshals are trained in traffic direction, conflict de-escalation, and emergency evacuation procedures.

Services include bay allocation management, visitor parking control, staff permit systems, and enforcement of parking policies. We install and maintain bay markings, signage, and barrier systems, and can integrate parking data with access control systems for ticketless, seamless entry and exit.

For high-footfall retail and hospitality clients, we deploy peak-time traffic management plans that reduce congestion, improve arrival experience, and reduce incidents. Monthly parking utilisation reports give you the data to optimise your car park layout and policy — reducing friction for your visitors and staff.

Energy Management

Bestcare FM's energy management service helps Kenyan businesses cut utility costs, reduce carbon footprint, and meet increasingly stringent NEMA environmental reporting requirements. We begin every energy engagement with a structured ISO 50001-aligned energy audit — surveying all major consumption points, identifying inefficiencies, and quantifying savings opportunities.

Typical interventions include LED lighting retrofits (reducing lighting energy by up to 65%), HVAC optimisation and BMS calibration, compressed air system audits, and solar PV feasibility assessments. Each intervention is costed, payback-modelled, and prioritised by ROI so you invest where it matters most.

Monthly energy dashboards show kWh consumption by floor, system, and time-of-use — giving facilities managers the granular data needed to challenge unusual spikes and validate savings. Clients on full energy management programmes have achieved average energy cost reductions of 18–22% within the first 12 months of engagement.

Helpdesk & CAFM

Bestcare FM operates a 24/7 helpdesk and computerised asset and fault management (CAFM) platform that gives clients real-time visibility of every service request, asset status, and SLA performance indicator across their entire portfolio.

Every fault is logged via phone, WhatsApp, or the client portal, allocated to the nearest qualified operative, and tracked through to completion — with automated SLA breach alerts escalated to your account manager in real time. Asset registers, PPM schedules, and service certificates are all stored in the CAFM system and accessible to authorised client users 24/7.

Monthly CAFM reports cover total jobs raised, jobs completed within SLA, average response and resolution times, asset downtime, and maintenance spend by category. This data drives our continuous improvement programme — reducing reactive maintenance costs and extending asset lifespans for our clients year on year.

Bestcare FM's ISO 9001 certification means that every service we deliver — from cleaning to CAFM to energy management — is governed by documented, audited processes that are consistently reviewed and improved.

ISO 14001:2015 — Environmental Management

ISO 14001:2015 is the international standard for environmental management systems (EMS). Bestcare FM's certification demonstrates our commitment to systematically managing our environmental impact — covering waste generation, chemical usage, energy consumption, and carbon emissions across our operations.
